LAIzy Teach | AI supporting education

LAIzyTeach makes teaching easy and effortless by using advanced artificial intelligence to create simple and clear teaching materials based on entered sources. The user simply needs to enter the source materials of their choice, based on which the artificial intelligence creates the presentation materials in minutes. The program is designed to create materials that are as easy to understand as possible and our service also offers extensive tools for editing materials to meet each user's own needs.

More time for teaching

Say goodbye to stress and let artificial intelligence make your life easier. We want to make teachers' everyday life easier and make teaching and learning effortless. The ability of teachers to cope makes teaching higher quality and that is a great advantage for students as well!
