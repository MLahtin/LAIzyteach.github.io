About Us

Our company started in 2022 from an idea received in a group of five people. The new possibilities brought by artificial intelligence piqued our interest. At that time, there was a lot of news about the challenges and exhaustion of teachers, and through that we got the idea to develop an artificial intelligence program that could help teachers.

2024 The LAIzyTeach program was completed and ready for the market. During the test phases, we received a lot of positive feedback from teachers and students of secondary schools and universities. The materials made by LAIzyTeach were clear, easy to understand and easy to edit. The program received praise from teachers for its functionality, but above all for the additional time it provided. There was more time for teaching itself, which the students also gave good feedback on. The program also gave teachers more free time when they could take better care of their mental well-being.

LAIzyTeach is personally important project for us, which we will continue to develope even further, so the future of teachers and the future generations would be better.
