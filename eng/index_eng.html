What is LAIzyTeach?

LAIzyTeach is a Finnish company that develops an artificial intelligence program what creates teaching and presentation materials. The LAIzyTeach artificial intelligence program analyzes the source materials from teachers, and based on them makes presentation materials that can be used in teaching. Our goal is to make teachers' everyday life easier by reducing the time spent on creating materials. The well-being of teachers and thus high-quality teaching is a matter of our hearts. But we want to reach this goal without shortcuts. That is why the artificial intelligence we have developed has been created with very high standards, so that the level of teaching remains as high as possible. LAIzyTeach saves time, and gives teachers more time for high-quality teaching and gives them more free time. Teachers well-being helps us to create better future, and keeps teaching at a high quality!
